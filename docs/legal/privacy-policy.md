# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2026

## 1. Introduction

Undash-cop ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Metrics Billing Platform ("the Platform", "the Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, organization details, billing information
- **Usage Data**: Events, metrics, and usage data submitted through the Platform
- **Payment Information**: Processed through Razorpay (we do not store payment card details)
- **Communication Data**: Emails, support tickets, and other communications

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics**: API usage patterns, request frequency, error logs
- **Technical Data**: IP addresses, browser type, device information
- **Cookies**: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience

### 2.3 Third-Party Information
- **Payment Data**: Razorpay provides payment transaction data
- **Email Service Data**: Email delivery status from email service providers

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- **Provide the Service**: Process events, generate invoices, manage billing
- **Process Payments**: Facilitate payment processing through Razorpay
- **Communicate**: Send invoices, payment confirmations, service updates
- **Improve Service**: Analyze usage patterns to improve the Platform
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
- **Compliance**: Meet legal and regulatory requirements
- **Support**: Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage
- **Primary Storage**: Amazon RDS (PostgreSQL) for financial and billing data
- **Event Storage**: Cloudflare D1 for hot event storage (7-day retention)
- **PDF Storage**: Cloudflare R2 for invoice PDFs
- **Geographic Location**: Data is stored in India and may be replicated for redundancy

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: Data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access control (RBAC) and API key authentication
- **Audit Logging**: All administrative actions are logged
- **Regular Backups**: Automated backups of critical data
- **Security Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring for security threats

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained while your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: Data may be retained for up to 7 years for legal compliance
- **Event Data**: D1 events are automatically deleted after 7 days
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion, except where retention is required by law

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Service Providers
We share data with trusted third-party service providers:
- **Razorpay**: Payment processing (subject to Razorpay's privacy policy)
- **Cloudflare**: Infrastructure and hosting services
- **Amazon Web Services**: Database hosting and storage
- **Email Providers**: SendGrid, Resend, or AWS SES for email delivery

### 5.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to government requests
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud or security threats
- Protect the safety of users

### 5.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

### 5.4 With Your Consent
We may share your information with your explicit consent.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Correction
- You can access and update your account information through the Platform
- You can request a copy of your data by contacting us

### 6.2 Data Portability
- You can export your usage data and invoices
- We provide APIs for programmatic access to your data

### 6.3 Deletion
- You can request deletion of your account and data
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance

### 6.4 Opt-Out
- You can opt out of marketing emails (transactional emails cannot be opted out)
- You can disable email notifications in your account settings

### 6.5 Cookies
- You can control cookies through your browser settings
- Disabling cookies may affect Platform functionality

## 7. International Data Transfers
